Warehousing

Our warehousing service includes 3 main steps:

We contact the supplier on behalf of you to communicate the delivery details.

  • We can even help you pay them, or finish the sourcing and purchase, if needed, in this step.

We pick up the goods from supplier/ manufacturer to our warehouse.

  • The goods can be picked up no matter where the manufactures are in China.

We collect the goods in our warehouse, store them and report the inventory data to you.

  • We have E-commerce fulfilment warehouse, general/bonded transship warehouse. And there are different warehouses and different locations, including in China, US & UK
  • China's warehouse is closer to the suppliers/factories/manufactures, so it is more convenient to transship the goods from/to them, if needed.

Fulfillment

Connectco provides fulfilment services which include, among others: receipt of an order, invoicing, production, printing, storage, packaging, packing, shipment and handling returns.

  • Your e-store is orders data transferred to our fulfillment center warehouse.
  • We sort out the suitable SKU product.
  • We pick and pack the product by new carton/bag.
  • We print and stick the related label.
  • We prepare the cartons/parcels in our warehouse for next shipping arrangement.

Global sourcing

Connectco has a global connection which ensures the best hands handle your sourcing, production, shipment, and fulfillment. We’ll help you find, choose, and manage suppliers to bring your product to the market. We can help you source/purchase products from China's suppliers. Meantime, we can help you pay them, check their products availability, delivery date, etc.

The process of sourcing from China, USA, Turkey & other markets involve:

Request for Quote

We provide you with a minimum of 3 written sealed quotes from different suppliers

Production process

We place the order in your name to the company while applying for terms and conditions. We also discuss all details such as payment term, delivery, quantity while pushing for quick delivery.

Quality Control

We monitor the entire order during the production process and inspect all your goods before shipment to ensure they are in perfect order.

Delivered to your doorstep

Whether you want the goods to be shipped to our warehouse or to your doorstep, we’ll handle the logistics for you. We take care of the entire shipping and customs clearance process from USA, China & Turkey to Kuwait.
